toolove for MacOS

curl -sS https://toolove.falseinput.com/toolove.sh | sh

Installs shit you need anyway:

  • Brew
  • Ghostty
  • Nerd fonts
  • Starship
  • Mise

It automatically configures .zshrc to use Starship prompt & load Mise context.

After installation, reload you shell: source ~/.zshrc
